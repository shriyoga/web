About Shri Yoga

A community-driven non-profit dedicated to making yoga free, accessible, and transformative for everyone.

Our Mission

Shri Yoga is a calming but challenging yoga style practiced in an informal group environment — open to everyone, always free.

🤝

Community First

All classes free and open to everyone — children to seniors, beginners to advanced — hosted at community spaces and volunteer homes.

🌍

Accessible to All

From local Maryland classes to a global virtual platform, we bring yoga to anyone who wants it, wherever they are.

💖

Selfless Service

Driven entirely by volunteer teachers and generous donations — a true community of love, compassion, and shared wellbeing.

Our Story

From local Maryland classes to a global yoga family.

2012

Local Classes Begin

Shri Ji starts teaching free yoga at local temples, community halls, and volunteer homes, reaching ~300 students in the Maryland tri-state area.

2020

Going Virtual

COVID-19 response: Shri Ji forms a 501(c)(3) non-profit and launches a virtual platform — growing from 300 to 1,200+ students in the USA and internationally.

2021–22

Community Grows

Platform expands to 70+ weekly classes with 30+ certified volunteer teachers. Kids Yoga, Meditation, Pranayama, and specialty classes added.

2024

In-Person Returns

In-person classes resume in Clarksburg and SSVT, Maryland. New Osteoporosis and Acupressure programs launched.

Today

A Global Yoga Family

A thriving community of students, teachers, and supporters united by yoga, wellness, and selfless service.

Founder & Lead Teacher

RYT 200 Certified · 500+ Hours Training
Founder & Lead Instructor

Shrikant "Shri Ji" Sabnis

Fondly known as "Shri Ji" and "Shri Kaka," Shrikant Sabnis retired after 40 years in safety-critical engineering to dedicate his life to teaching yoga and spreading love and compassion in his community.

For over 12 years, he has conducted free yoga at temples, community halls, volunteer homes, and online — traveling 70 miles a day and growing from local classes to 1,200+ global students.

  • ✅ Certified RYT 200 — OneAum Studio, Potomac MD
  • ✅ 500+ hours yoga teacher training
  • ✅ Trained in Sudarshan Kriya, Vipassana, 3-Step Rhythmic Breathing
  • ✅ Inspired 30+ certified practitioners
  • ✅ Featured by Voice of America

Classes: Regular, Pranayama, Intermediate, Meditation, Kids Yoga

Before You Join

👕

What to Wear

Comfortable, stretchy clothing. Yoga pants, leggings, or shorts. Bare feet preferred.

🎒

What to Bring

Yoga mat, water bottle, small towel. Online: stable internet & enough floor space.

🍽️

Before & After

Avoid heavy meals 2 hours before. Stay hydrated. Rest briefly after class.
