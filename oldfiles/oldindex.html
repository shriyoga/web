Meet Our Founder

Shri Sabnis

Fondly known as "Shri Ji" and "Shri Kaka," Shrikant Sabnis retired after 40 years in safety-critical engineering to dedicate his life to teaching yoga and spreading love and compassion.

For over 12 years, he has conducted free yoga classes at temples, community halls, volunteer homes, and online — growing to 1,200+ students worldwide.

  • ✅ Certified RYT 200 — Vinyasa yoga
  • ✅ 500+ hours of teacher training
  • ✅ Trained in Sudarshan Kriya & Vipassana
  • ✅ Inspired 30+ certified practitioners
